Time Complexities in Ascending Order
1. `1`
2. `log n`
3. `sqrt n`
4. `n`
5. `n log n`
6. `n ^ 2`
7. `2 ^ n`
8. `n ^  n`

Relationship may not hold at small values of `n`.
* However, as `n` approaches infinity, will always be true.
* ex. `n ^ 100` and `2 ^ n`
